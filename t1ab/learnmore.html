Smart Homes

Learn how IoT is transforming homes into smart homes.

What are Smart Homes?

A smart home is a residence that uses internet-connected devices to enable the remote monitoring and management of appliances and systems, such as lighting and heating. These devices are often controlled through a smartphone app or voice-controlled assistant like Amazon's Alexa or Google Home.

How do Smart Homes work?

Smart homes use a variety of sensors, devices, and other internet-connected technologies to automate and optimize various functions within the home. These technologies can range from simple lightbulbs and thermostats to more complex security systems and home entertainment systems.
